Depends on scope. Small tools/features: 2-4 weeks. Full applications: 2-4 months. Curriculum development: 1-3 months. AI integration: 1-2 months. Timelines established during scoping phase.

Yes. All projects include initial support period (typically 30 days). Extended support and maintenance available via retainer or hourly arrangements.

Absolutely. Experienced integrating with D2L, Moodle, Canvas, various APIs, legacy systems. Can work within your existing technology stack or recommend modern alternatives.
