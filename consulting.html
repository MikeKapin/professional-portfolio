Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work remotely or on-site?

Primarily remote work with flexibility for on-site visits when needed (Ontario region). Most projects can be completed entirely remotely with video calls for collaboration.

What's your typical project timeline?

Depends on scope. Small tools/features: 2-4 weeks. Full applications: 2-4 months. Curriculum development: 1-3 months. AI integration: 1-2 months. Timelines established during scoping phase.

Do you provide ongoing support after delivery?

Yes. All projects include initial support period (typically 30 days). Extended support and maintenance available via retainer or hourly arrangements.

Absolutely. Experienced integrating with various APIs, databases, legacy systems, and existing platforms. Can work within your existing technology stack or recommend modern alternatives.

Hourly: Invoiced bi-weekly or monthly. Project-based: Milestone payments (typically 30% deposit, progress payments, final upon delivery). Retainer: Monthly invoicing. All major payment methods accepted.
