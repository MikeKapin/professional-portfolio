About

A builder, quietly.

I'm Mike. I live in Ontario, teach at a local college as a program coordinator in the skilled trades, and spend a lot of my non-work time building software.

Most of what I make starts with a problem I'm trying to solve for myself — tracking my own health, managing a complicated co-parenting schedule, quoting contractor work, figuring out how to use AI in my own workflow. When something turns out well enough that other people might find it useful, I put it here.

This site is personal. Nothing on it is for sale. There are no accounts to create, no paywalls, no pitch. If a tool here helps you, take it and use it.
