From Frustration to Innovation

After witnessing the challenges students faced navigating complex HVAC codes and certification requirements, and recognizing the potential of AI to transform technical education, LARK Labs was born from a simple question: "What if technology could make learning technical trades as intuitive as having an expert mentor at your side?"
